About Sweet Crumbs Bakery

A family tradition of baking, served with passion since day one

Our Story

Sweet Crumbs Bakery began in 2012 as a small family kitchen with a single oven and a big dream: to bring honest, handcrafted baked goods to our neighbourhood. What started as weekend bread sales at the local farmers market has grown into a beloved community bakery, but our values have never changed. Every cake, loaf, and pastry is still made by hand, in small batches, using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

M

Our Mission

To bake exceptional, honest food using quality ingredients, and to share it with our community through warm, personal service every single day.

V

Our Vision

To be the neighbourhood's most trusted bakery — a place where every celebration, big or small, begins with something freshly baked.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing an order

Do you make custom cakes?+

Yes! We specialize in custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and corporate events. Simply contact us with your design ideas, flavor preferences, and event date, and our team will create a personalized quote for you.

How can I place an order?+

Orders can be placed through our contact form, by phone, or in person at our bakery counter. For custom cakes, we recommend reaching out as early as possible to discuss design and flavor options.

Do you offer delivery?+

Yes, we offer local delivery within a 10-mile radius of our bakery for a small fee. Delivery availability and timing depend on order size and the date requested, so please confirm details when placing your order.

What payment methods are accepted?+

We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, and popular digital payment methods. Custom orders typically require a deposit at the time of booking, with the balance due upon pickup or delivery.

How many days in advance should I order?+

For everyday items like bread and pastries, no advance notice is needed. For custom cakes and large orders, we recommend placing your order at least 3 to 5 days in advance, and 2 to 3 weeks for wedding cakes during busy seasons.

Do you cater events and office parties?+

Absolutely! We offer event catering with custom pastry platters, cake displays, and bread selections. Our team will work with you to create a spread that impresses your guests and suits your budget.

Are any of your products gluten-free or vegan?+

We offer a small selection of gluten-free and vegan-friendly baked goods. Please let us know about any dietary requirements when placing your order so we can accommodate you.
